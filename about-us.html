Our Story

Hygenix Seeds is the brainchild of Shri Yaganti Venkateswarlu (YVR), a visionary with over 40 years of expertise in the seed industry. His extensive experience, particularly in developing field crop hybrids, has established him as a leader in creating seeds that stand out for their quality and performance in the market.

Known for his commitment to quality, YVR ensures that both farmers and trade partners benefit from superior seeds at accessible prices. The YVR Group's vast network of 1,800 distributors and dealers across the country is a testament to the trust and respect the brand has earned.
